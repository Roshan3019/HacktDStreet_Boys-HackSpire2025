Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, premium support customers receive priority assistance.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes, phone support is available for Business and Enterprise plan customers. Standard plan users can schedule a call with our support team as needed.

Can I request a product demo?

Absolutely! You can request a personalized demo through our contact form. Select "Product Demo" as the subject, and our sales team will arrange a convenient time.

How do I report a bug?

For bug reports, please select "Technical Support" in the contact form and provide detailed information about the issue, including steps to reproduce, screenshots, and your account details.
