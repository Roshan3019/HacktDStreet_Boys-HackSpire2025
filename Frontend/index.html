Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QueueWise Pro.

Our AI-powered predictions are typically accurate within ±10% of actual wait times. The system continuously learns from historical data, current conditions, and staff availability to provide the most accurate estimates possible. As more data is collected, the predictions become even more accurate over time.

Booking a slot is simple: select your desired service center, choose an available time slot (with AI recommendations for less busy times), enter your details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with a QR code that you can scan upon arrival to check in quickly without waiting in line.

If you miss your booked slot, the system will hold your position for 10 minutes after your scheduled time. After that, your booking will be automatically canceled, and you'll need to make a new booking or join the regular queue. We recommend arriving 5 minutes before your scheduled time for the best experience.
